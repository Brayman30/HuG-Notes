    # Types of Population Density

    Arithmatic Density - $\frac{\text{Population}}{\text{Ammount Of Land}}$ Shows how spread out people are. Physilogical Density - $\frac{\text{Population}}{\text{Arable Land}}$ Shows how much stress is being put on the land. Agricultural Density - $\frac{\text{Number of Farmers}}{\text{Arable Land}}$ Shows how andvanced the society is, the lower the number, the more people are free to do other jobs that aren’t farming.

    # Population Density vs Distubition

    Population Density - How many people are living in this one spot. Population Distributuion - How are people located. Density allows geographers to understand a lot about a state.

    # Carrying Capacity

    How many people can live in an area without causing harm to the enviroment. If the carrying capacity is exceeded, things will go real bad, real quick.
